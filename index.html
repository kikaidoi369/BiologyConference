Asian Society of Arachnology Conference

WELCOME

The Asian Society of Arachnology (ASA) invites you to join us at the 5th ASA conference in Chulalongkorn University (CU), Bangkok, Thailand.

The conference will be held during the 19th –22nd November 2018 in one of the most prestigious research institutions in Thailand, Chulalongkorn University in the city of Bangkok. Bangkok is the capital and most populous city in Thailand and among the world top tourist destinations. Participants can arrive in one of the two international airports available: Don Mueang airport and Suvarnabhumi airport.

The conference venue will be hosted at the Chaloem Rajakumari 60 Building (AKA Chamchuri 10) located in CU beautiful main campus with accessibility to major public transportations to get to most major tourist attractions, accommodations, and foods in Bangkok. A one-day excursion (21st November) will bring you to King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi, Ratchaburi campus, Ratchaburi province. The vast landscape of the campus (over 20+ hectares) covers primary dipterocarp forest along with secondary and tertiary successions adjacent (less than 50 km) to the border of Myanmar. You will have the experience of encountering arachnids in a tropical seasonal rain forest.

We look forward to welcoming you in 2018 in Bangkok!
