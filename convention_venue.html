CONVENTION VENUE AND HOW TO GET THERE

Chamchuri10 Building

All parts of the scientific program will be hosted at the Chaloem Rajakumari 60 Building (AKA Chamchuri 10; find more information at www.prm.chula.ac.th) located in CU main campus.

Chamchuri 10 building is primarily used for international studies and graduate school, but has been routinely used for numerous conferences held by CU.

The main auditorium conference room on 8th floor with a maximum capacity of holding 200 guests will be used for oral and poster presentations.

Chaloem Rajakumari 60 Building (Chamchuri 10)

How to get there ?

To get to the conference venue, participants should arrive in one of the two Thailand international airports available: Don Mueang and Suvarnabhumi airports.

From Don Mueang Airport (https://www.donmueangairport.com): there couple of routes that you can explore depending on whether how adventurous you are and if you want to save some money.

  • Route 1
    At the arrival terminal there is a free airport shuttle bus number A1 that will take you to Mo Chit (the largest bus terminal in Bangkok). As you arrive at Mo Chit, which is near a sky train (BTS:http://www.bts.co.th) station, you can take the sky train to arrive at Siam station. Departing the station, you can flag down a taxi and tell the driver you are heading for Chamchuri 10 building in CU.
  • Route 2
    There is a taxi station in Don Mueang Airport, you have to wait for a cue and pay addition 50 Baht surcharge on top of what the meter say when you arrive at CU.
  • Route 3
    Airport limousine xxxx

From Suvarnabhumi Airport (https://airportthai.co.th): Suvarnabhumi airport provide more convenient way of traveling to CU. There are such options.

  • Route 1
    The lowest floor of Suvarnabhumi airport, there is an access to the Airport Rail Link (ARL http://www.srtet.co.th/index.php/en/), a train connecting from the airport to the inner city of Bangkok. The last stop for the ARL is your destination (Phaya Thai station). From the station, you can flag down a taxi and tell the driver you are heading for Chamchuri 10 building in CU.
  • Route 2
    A taxi service is available in level xx of the airport. You have to wait for a cue and pay addition 50 Baht surcharge on top of what the meter say when you arrive at CU
  • Route 3
    Airport limousine xxxx
