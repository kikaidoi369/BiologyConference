King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi

A one-day excursion on the 21st November will bring you to King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi (KMUTT), Ratchaburi campus.

The landscape of the excursion area covers primary dipterocarp forest along with secondary and tertiary succession zones of over 20+ hectares. KMUTT, Ratchaburi campus is adjacent (less than 50 km) to the border of Myanmar, where pristine nature is still preserved.

You will have the experience of encountering spiders and other arachnids in seasonal rain forest of Southeast Asia.

Bus transporting delegates to KMUTT, Ratchaburi campus will leave at 7:00 from the Montein Hotel Bangkok (see ACCOMODATION) and will leave KMUTT at 17:00. The traveling time from CU to KMUTT is approximately 2 hours.
