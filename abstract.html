ABSTRACT

Submission of abstract for the 5th ASA conference is invited for both oral and poster presentations. Before doing so, please carefully read all of the guidelines for a successful abstract submission. The organizers reserved the authority to accept or reject submission that do not meet with all of our guidelines. Revision of delegate abstract may be required based on decision of the organizing committee.

Guideline for oral presentation

  • Abstract must be submitted online by 15th October 2018 Thailand time
  • Abstract should be written in English
  • Abstract cannot contain special symbols, diagrams, photographs or tables
  • The abstract title should not exceed 20 words
  • The abstract text should not exceed 2,000 characters (including space)
  • Abstract submission must be exclusively done online through xxxx
  • Notification for oral presentation will be confirmed after two weeks of abstract submission
  • All presenters MUST register for the conference
